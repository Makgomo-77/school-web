Frequently Asked Questions

Regular school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM for all grades. Early drop-off is available starting at 7:30 AM, and after-school care is available until 6:00 PM for an additional fee.

Please call the school office at 063 937 4786 by 8:30 AM to report your child's absence. You may also email attendance@brightfuture.edu with your child's name, grade, and reason for absence.

Bright Future Academy has a standardized dress code. Solid color polo shirts (navy, white, or light blue) with khaki or navy pants, skirts, or shorts. Complete details are available in the school handbook.

We welcome parent involvement! Opportunities include the PTA, classroom volunteering, chaperoning field trips, and assisting with special events. Contact the front office or your child's teacher to learn more.

We offer free peer tutoring after school on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Teachers are available for extra help during designated office hours. Additionally, we maintain a list of recommended private tutors upon request.
